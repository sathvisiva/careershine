About Career Shine Academy

Career Shine Academy, as an education partner of Hughes Global Education, offers executive programs directly from IIMs, IITs, and other prestigious institutions. Career Shine Academy is partnership firm established on May 2015 at East Tambaram- Chennai. Main objective of Career Shine Academy is to provide the best B-schools executive programmes for the professionals residing around Tambaram -Chennai Circle.


Hughes Global Education is the pioneer in providing the ‘Interactive Online Learning Platform’ for the working professionals to pursue an executive mba. The programs are provided by the top notch B-schools viz.IIM Calcutta, IIM Kozhikode, IIM Rohtak, IIFT New Delhi and XLRI Jamshedpur. The best faculties in the country take live lectures imparting knowledge that is exclusive with specializations across various streams like General Management, Sales & Marketing, Human Resource, Project Management, Finance, International Trade etc


IIM Executive Education programmes have been designed to effectively spread quality management education among practicing managers, across all levels. These programmes equip experienced professionals with powerful knowledge, tools and resources to tackle the most complex business challenges of today.


The study material provided to students is prepared by the concerned school from which the program has been opted. The certification upon successful completion of the course comes from the institution. The center facilitates an online interactive virtual classroom, which is transmission of the action in real time at the concerned institute. The delivery mode is a satellite-based online education platform designed by Hughes Global Education.

PEDAGOGY

At the core of Career Shine Academy’s educational delivery methodology is the state-of-the-art Interactive Onsite Learning (IOL) model developed by Hughes Global Education. Students attend classes being beamed in real time through satellite. Live video of action at the institute’s classroom is captured on camera and transmitted live to all Hughes centers throughout India. The lecture being conducted by the institute faculty members is thus relayed in remote classrooms created through dedicated broadband two-way audio-visual equipment. The technology has several benefits over face-to-face teaching. Students can use a virtual raise hand-feature seeking-teacher's attention and tabulate responses to quizzes randomly created by the teacher.


The methodology enables peer-to-peer collaboration among students. Students can discuss about projects within a centre or across different centres. It also enables individual assignments to students.

TEACHING-LEARNING METHODOLOGY

Career Shine Academy offers highest level of interactivity in during its virtual learning sessions. Combined with the Interactive Onsite Learning (IOL) platform, the teaching-learning methodology offers several benefits that far surpass the learning experience of a live classroom.


The first benefit of the IOL learning is that it combines in class-management tools that help the faculty in conducting the class effectively. Also available are built-in measurement and certification tools that help the faculty to monitor each student's progress accurately and effectively. It is an ideal way to to increase the reach and coverage of premier institutions without compromising on interactivity, control and certification, and quality of content.


Combine that with best of the faculty teaching and ‘The faculties’ - the best faculty can now centrally reach and address participants simultaneously in various cities across the country.
